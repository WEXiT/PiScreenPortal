Presentation mode (AirPlay)

Share your iPhone, iPad or Mac screen to the Raspberry via AirPlay. The kiosk is paused during presentation.

How to connect

  1. iPhone / iPad: open Control Center, tap Screen Mirroring, choose PiScreenPortal.
  2. Mac: Control Center → Screen Mirroring → PiScreenPortal.
  3. Windows / Android: AirPlay is not natively supported. Recommended: Deskreen or AirMyPC.
